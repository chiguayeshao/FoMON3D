### English

FoMON3D is an innovative Fomo3D game designed to raise funds for the Agent Treasury. Users deposit `$MON` to receive `$FoMON` tokens, which represent their share in the Agent Treasury and give them a chance to win a substantial prize pool.

**Core Mechanics:**

- **Agent Treasury Investment Strategy**: The Agent Treasury uses collected `$MON` to invest in launchpads such as nad.fun. By investing in newly launched projects and executing TWAP exits, the treasury generates nearly risk-free returns. With its own analytical capabilities and preferences, the Agent Treasury selects the most promising MEME projects for investment to maximize success rates.

- **Game Rules**: Users deposit `$MON` to receive `$FoMON`. If no one deposits `$MON` within 30 seconds, the last depositor wins and earns the majority of the Agent Treasury's returns.

- **Flexible Exit Options**:
  - If you don't win, you can choose to hold `$FoMON` or redeem it back to `$MON`
  - In most cases, there will be no loss, but you need to enter a 7-day exit queue
  - If a DEX exists in the market, users can also sell `$FoMON` directly
